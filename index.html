ABOUT THE TRUST

Vallal P.T.Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker, the illustrious founder of this institution was born on 10th April 1829. The trust was started in the year 1874 in the name of P.T. Lee Chengalvaraya Naicker conceived by benevolent man mainly for the purpose to imparting free education and free medical services for economically weaker and downtrodden society. As per the orders of Hon'ble High Court of Madras, the trust started functioning as a separate entity from the year 1996. Our trust is being administered by a team of nine eminent trustees drawn from different walks of life and who were holding very high positions in the past. Presently the trust board is headed by Hon'ble Justice P.Kalaiyarasan, former Judge, High Court of Madras as Chairman and other trustees duly appointed by Government of Tamil Nadu
